After a decade working in Toronto's performing arts scene designing ads, making videos, taking photos, drafting social media posts (and whatever else that was needed), I've spent the past few months of the pandemic with the cool people at Juno College learning to code... at home in my sweatpants. I'm a dog dad and occasional DJ with a love of minimalist design, electronic music and science fiction. Wanna talk about web dev projects, synthesizers or Star Trek? Let's connect!
